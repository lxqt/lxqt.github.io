The LXQt team announces the release of lxqt-session 2.4.0.
The release can be downloaded from [Github](https://github.com/lxqt/lxqt-session/releases).

Main changes:

 * Separated X11 and Wayland settings.
 * Don't include Qt modules directly.
 * Updated translations.


<br/>
A full list of changes is in the CHANGELOG file.
