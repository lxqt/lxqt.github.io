The LXQt team announces the release of xdg-desktop-portal-lxqt 1.4.0
The release can be downloaded from [Github](https://github.com/lxqt/xdg-desktop-portal-lxqt/releases).

Changes:

 * Added support for setting parent window on Wayland.
 * Added access portal and refactored shared choice types.
