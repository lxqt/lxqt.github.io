The LXQt team announces a point release of **lxqt-wayland-session 0.3.2**


Changes:

 * Updated configurations to cover labwc 0.9.5 and Hyprland 0.54.1.
 * Enabled launch of lxqt-leave by pressing the powerbutton in niri configuration.
 * Fixed hyprland config for breaking changes of 0.53.
  
<br/>
The release can be downloaded from [Github](https://github.com/lxqt/lxqt-wayland-session/releases).
