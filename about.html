The focused classic with a modern look and feel.

An unhindered and clear way to go.

  • Get the full bundle or only the basics
  • Enjoy the friendly file manager
  • Customize the look and feel
  • Configure panels and plugins
  • Use any window manager you want

Applications

  • lxqt-runner — Launch applications and do calculations
  • PCManFM-Qt — Browse files
  • LXImage-Qt — View images
  • QTerminal — Use the terminal
  • ScreenGrab — Capture screenshots
  • LXQt Archiver — Manage archives
  • Qps — Manage processes

Try LXQt in most Linux or BSD distributions.
Find out how on the LXQt wiki.

Read about its history in the LXQt wiki.
